# Custody Agreements for Physical Asset Backing

## Overview

This document outlines custody agreements and procedures for ensuring the physical backing of tokenized assets on the Stellar blockchain. Proper custody arrangements are essential for maintaining the value and integrity of tokenized real-world assets.

---

## 1. General Custody Framework

### 1.1 Custodian Selection Criteria
- **Financial Stability:** Minimum net worth requirements and insurance coverage
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Proper licensing and regulatory oversight
- **Experience:** Proven track record in asset custody
- **Security:** Robust security protocols and insurance
- **Auditability:** Regular third-party audits and reporting

### 1.2 Custodial Responsibilities
- **Physical Storage:** Secure storage of physical assets
- **Insurance:** Comprehensive insurance coverage
- **Audit:** Regular physical and financial audits
- **Reporting:** Periodic reporting to token holders
- **Redemption:** Processing physical asset redemptions

### 1.3 Risk Mitigation
- **Diversified Storage:** Multiple geographic locations
- **Insurance Coverage:** All-risk insurance policies
- **Third-Party Verification:** Independent auditors
- **Legal Agreements:** Legally binding custody contracts
- **Contingency Planning:** Emergency procedures

## 2. Asset-Specific Custody Agreements

### 2.1 Precious Metals (Gold, Silver, Platinum)

#### 2.1.1 Custodian Requirements
- **Licensed Vault Operators:** Registered with relevant authorities
- **Segregated Storage:** Allocated and segregated storage
- **Insurance:** Full replacement value insurance
- **Audit Frequency:** Quarterly physical audits
- **Redemption:** Physical delivery procedures

#### 2.1.2 Storage Specifications
- **Vault Class:** Class III vaults or equivalent
- **Security:** 24/7 armed security, motion detectors
- **Environmental:** Climate-controlled, fire suppression
- **Location:** Geographically dispersed vaults
- **Accessibility:** Reasonable redemption access

#### 2.1.3 Sample Agreement Terms
```
CUSTODY AGREEMENT - PRECIOUS METALS

1. Asset Description: [Metal Type], [Weight], [Purity]
2. Storage Location: [Vault Facility], [Address]
3. Insurance: Full replacement value, [Insurance Provider]
4. Audit Schedule: Quarterly physical verification
5. Fees: [Annual Fee]% of asset value
6. Redemption: [Notice Period] days for physical delivery
7. Liability: Custodian liable for loss, theft, damage
```

### 2.2 Real Estate Properties

#### 2.2.1 Property Management Requirements
- **Licensed Property Managers:** Professional management companies
- **Maintenance:** Regular maintenance and repairs
- **Insurance:** Property and liability insurance
- **Tenant Management:** Lease administration and collection
- **Financial Reporting:** Monthly financial statements

#### 2.2.2 Legal Structure
- **Title Holding:** SPV holds legal title
- **Beneficial Interest:** Token holders hold beneficial interest
- **Liens:** No additional liens without token holder approval
- **Encumbrances:** Clear title insurance required

#### 2.2.3 Sample Agreement Terms
```
PROPERTY MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT

1. Property Address: [Full Property Address]
2. Management Fee: [Fee]% of gross rental income
3. Lease Administration: [Fee]% of collected rent
4. Maintenance Reserve: [Amount] monthly reserve
5. Reporting: Monthly financial and operational reports
6. Insurance: Comprehensive property insurance required
7. Termination: [Notice Period] days notice
```

### 2.3 Commodities (Oil, Gas, Agricultural Products)

#### 2.3.1 Storage Requirements
- **Approved Facilities:** Licensed storage facilities
- **Quality Control:** Regular quality testing
- **Inventory Management:** Real-time inventory tracking
- **Transportation:** Secure transportation arrangements
- **Insurance:** Full value insurance during storage and transport

#### 2.3.2 Quality Assurance
- **Grading:** Independent quality grading
- **Certification:** Industry-standard certifications
- **Testing:** Regular quality testing
- **Documentation:** Complete documentation trail
- **Verification:** Third-party verification

#### 2.3.3 Sample Agreement Terms
```
COMMODITY CUSTODY AGREEMENT

1. Commodity: [Commodity Type], [Grade], [Quantity]
2. Storage Facility: [Facility Name], [Location]
3. Quality Standards: [Industry Standard] certification
4. Testing Schedule: [Frequency] quality testing
5. Insurance: Full value insurance coverage
6. Transportation: Arranged by [Party]
7. Fees: [Storage Fee] per unit per month
```

### 2.4 Art and Collectibles

#### 2.4.1 Specialized Storage Requirements
- **Climate Control:** Temperature and humidity control
- **Security:** Museum-grade security systems
- **Conservation:** Professional conservation services
- **Insurance:** Fine art insurance coverage
- **Display:** Optional display arrangements

#### 2.4.2 Provenance Management
- **Documentation:** Complete provenance documentation
- **Authentication:** Professional authentication
- **Photography:** High-resolution documentation
- **Cataloging:** Detailed condition reports
- **Restoration:** Professional restoration services

#### 2.4.3 Sample Agreement Terms
```
ART CUSTODY AGREEMENT

1. Artwork: [Title], [Artist], [Dimensions]
2. Storage: Climate-controlled facility
3. Security: 24/7 surveillance and armed guards
4. Insurance: Fine art insurance, [Coverage Amount]
5. Conservation: Annual conservation assessment
6. Display: [Display Options] available
7. Fees: [Annual Fee]% of appraised value
```

### 2.5 Carbon Credits

- **Verification:** Third-party verification of credits

- **Reporting:** Regular reporting on credit status

#### 2.5.2 Quality Assurance

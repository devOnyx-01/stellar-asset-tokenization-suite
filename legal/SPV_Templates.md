# SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) Formation Templates

This directory contains templates for forming Special Purpose Vehicles across different jurisdictions for tokenizing real-world assets.

## Overview

Special Purpose Vehicles are legal entities created to isolate financial risk and enable the tokenization of real-world assets while maintaining regulatory compliance.

## Jurisdiction-Specific Templates

### 1. Delaware C-Corporation (United States)

**File:** `delaware_c_corp_template.md`

**Best For:**
- Real estate assets
- Securities tokens
- Venture capital investments

**Key Features:**
- Limited liability protection
- Flexible corporate structure
- Tax transparency for pass-through taxation
- Well-established legal precedent

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 director
- Registered agent in Delaware
- Annual franchise tax ($175 minimum)
- Certificate of Incorporation

### 2. Cayman Islands Exempted Company

**File:** `cayman_exempted_company_template.md`

**Best For:**
- International investment funds
- Hedge fund structures
- Cross-border asset tokenization

**Key Features:**
- Tax neutrality (no corporate tax)
- Strong privacy protections
- Flexible corporate governance
- No exchange controls

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 director
- Registered office in Cayman Islands
- Annual government fee
- Memorandum and Articles of Association

### 3. Singapore Private Limited Company

**File:** `singapore_pvt_ltd_template.md`

**Best For:**
- Asian market exposure
- Commodity tokenization
- FinTech applications

**Key Features:**
- Pro-business regulatory environment
- Strong IP protection
- Tax incentives for qualifying activities
- Access to Asian markets

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 resident director
- Local registered address
- Paid-up capital (minimum S$1)
- ACRA registration

### 4. Luxembourg S.à r.l. (Société à responsabilité limitée)

**File:** `luxembourg_sarl_template.md`

**Best For:**
- Investment funds
- European market access
- Asset management vehicles

**Key Features:**
- EU regulatory passporting
- Favorable tax regime
- Strong asset protection
- Professional investor friendly

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 manager
- Registered office in Luxembourg
- Minimum capital (€12,000)
- Articles of Incorporation

### 5. British Virgin Islands Business Company

**File:** `bvi_business_company_template.md`

**Best For:**
- International holding companies
- Asset securitization
- Privacy-focused structures

**Key Features:**
- No corporate tax
- Confidential ownership
- Simple incorporation process
- No exchange controls

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 1 director
- Registered agent in BVI
- Annual license fee
- Memorandum and Articles of Association

## Asset-Specific Considerations

### Real Estate Tokenization

**Recommended Jurisdictions:**
- Delaware C-Corp (US properties)
- Singapore Pte Ltd (Asian properties)
- Luxembourg S.à r.l. (European properties)

**Special Requirements:**
- Property title transfer documentation
- Local real estate licensing
- Property management agreements
- Insurance coverage

### Commodity Tokenization

**Recommended Jurisdictions:**
- Cayman Islands (international commodities)
- Singapore (Asian commodities)
- BVI (global trading)

**Special Requirements:**
- Custody agreements with vault operators
- Quality certification documentation
- Physical redemption procedures
- Insurance for stored commodities

### Invoice Tokenization

**Recommended Jurisdictions:**
- Delaware C-Corp (US receivables)
- Luxembourg S.à r.l. (European invoices)
- Singapore Pte Ltd (Asian trade finance)

**Special Requirements:**
- Assignment of receivables agreements
- Credit insurance policies
- Servicing agreements
- Collection procedures

### Security Token Offerings

**Recommended Jurisdictions:**
- Delaware C-Corp (Reg D offerings)
- Singapore Pte Ltd (Asian investors)
- Luxembourg S.à r.l. (EU investors)

**Special Requirements:**
- Securities law compliance
- Investor accreditation verification
- Prospectus preparation
- Regulatory approvals

## Implementation Steps

### 1. Jurisdiction Selection
- Consider target investor base
- Evaluate tax implications
- Assess regulatory requirements
- Review operational costs

### 2. Entity Formation
- Engage local legal counsel
- Prepare incorporation documents
- Appoint registered agent/office
- Complete regulatory filings

### 3. Asset Transfer
- Execute asset transfer agreements
- Obtain necessary third-party consents
- Update public records
- Secure insurance coverage

### 4. Tokenization Setup
- Deploy smart contracts
- Establish token economics
- Create investor onboarding process
- Implement compliance procedures

### 5. Ongoing Compliance
- Annual reporting requirements
- Tax filings and payments
- Regulatory audits
- Investor reporting

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Legal Risks
- Engage experienced local counsel
- Maintain proper corporate formalities
- Keep accurate corporate records
- Monitor regulatory changes

### Operational Risks
- Implement robust internal controls
- Maintain adequate insurance coverage
- Establish contingency procedures
- Regular compliance audits

### Market Risks
- Diversify investor base
- Implement liquidity provisions
- Monitor market conditions
- Adjust token economics as needed
